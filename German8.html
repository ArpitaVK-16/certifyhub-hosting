Learning any language is incomplete without knowing its vocabulary. This is the time to learn more German words.


Before that, you need to know one thing...


In English there are three articles : a, an, the
In German, for 'the', there are three forms according to gender of the noun.


Gender of nouns as in English are somewhat different in German. (E.g. The watch is considered at 'female' German!) Interesting, isn't it? So always learns new words as they are given in the table below.

You will explore more such interesting facts soon!


Always remember following table:

Male Female Neutral
English The The The
German Der Die Das
