Greetings in German

Following are informal and formal Greetings in German and their meaning in English:


Informal Greetings Formal Greetings
Hallo - Hello Guten Morgen - Good Morning
Guten Nachmittag - Good Afternoon
Guten Abend - Good Evening
Guten Nacht - Good Night
Guten Tag - Good Day
Tschüss - Goodbye Auf Wiedersehen - See you again
Wie geht es dir? - How's it been? Wie geht es inhnen? - How's it been?
Wie geht's? - How's it been?
Es geht mir gut - I'm doing well Es geht mir gut - I'm doing well
Gut - Well / Good
Nicht gut - Not good
Und dir? - And you? Und ihnen? - And you?
Auch gut - Also good Auch gut - Also good


Note:


How to learn and remember these greetings?

Don't worry! The table has different colors indicating different meanings. So learn them one by one.
