Vowels in German:



As we know, a, e, i, o, u are the 5 vowels in English. Like that, in German, there are some additional vowels such as ä, ö and ü.
Along with these vowels mentioned above, German is previledged with combination of vowels:

e + i = ei (aai)   e.g. mein

a + u = au (aau)   e.g. auch

e + u = eu (oy)    e.g. euro

i + e = ie (ie)    e.g. Sie
