In the last module, we learnt how to construct simple German sentences using 'haben' and 'sein'.


So, now it's time to learn 'Self Introduction!'


Which points to include in self introduction?


  • Your Name (e.g. I am Aniket.)
  • Your profession (e.g. I am doctor.)
  • Your native place (e.g. I am from India.)
  • Your age (e.g. I am 31 years old.)
  • Languages you speak (e.g. I speak Marathi, English, Hindi, and Sanskrit.)



Let's translate above sentences into German.


  • I am Aniket. - Ich bin Aniket.
  • I am doctor. -Ich bin Arzt.
  • I am from India. -Ich komme aus India.
  • I am 31 years old. -Ich bin einunddreizig Jahre Alt.
  • I speak Marathi, English, Hindi, and Sanskrit. -Ich spreche Marathi, English, Hindi und Sanskrit.
